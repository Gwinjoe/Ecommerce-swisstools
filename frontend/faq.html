Frequently Asked Questions

What is the return policy for tools?

Learn about our 30-day return policy.

You can return tools within 30 days of purchase with a receipt, provided they are unused and in original packaging. Contact our support team for assistance.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times for Nigeria and international orders.

Shipping within Nigeria takes 3-7 business days. International shipping varies by location, typically 7-14 business days.

Do you offer warranties on equipment?

Details on equipment warranties.

Yes, most equipment comes with a 1-year manufacturer warranty. Check product details for specific warranty information.

How can I track my order?

Track your order with our tracking system.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. Use it on our website or the courier's portal to track your package.
